About the studio

We're builders on both sides of the screen.

Two Dreams Labs LLC is a small, independent game studio. We make our own games — and because we live in that pipeline every day, we build the tools that make creating, shipping and operating games faster and calmer for teams like ours.

That's the loop behind DreamCI, Mirage and Bennu: real problems from real game-making, turned into infrastructure anyone can use.

Dogfooded

Our own games run on our tools. If it isn't good enough for us, it doesn't ship to you.

No lock-in

Open standards where it counts — Actions-compatible workflows, portable projects, your data.

Secure by design

Multi-tenant with row-level security, encrypted secrets, scoped keys and full audit logs.

Where we are now

Technically deep, and entering private beta.

DreamCI already has a complete dashboard, RBAC, billing scaffolding, audit, live logs and a multi-tenant architecture. Mirage and Bennu are in active development. We're hardening everything for production — want to pilot with your studio? We'd love to talk.
